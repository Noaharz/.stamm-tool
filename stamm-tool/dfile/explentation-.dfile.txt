.dfile is an format for digital profiles. It looks like this:
    !=! (start)
    NAME: Max Mustermann
    GEBURT: 1980-01-01
    STERBEDATUM: 
    GENDER: M
    GEBURTSORT: Hamburg
    VERSION: 1
    NOTIZ: examplenote
    FOTO: 
    !0! (end)
    
Check the official .dfile repo on GitHub for better informations.
